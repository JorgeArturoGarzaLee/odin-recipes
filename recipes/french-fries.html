"FRENCH FRIES"

the most scrumptious, crunchy and comforting brown food for your child-like palette taken to the extremes of crunch.

ingredients:

  • 3 large russet potatoes
  • 1 and a 1/2 tbsp distilled white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt + more to taste
  • 3 qt vegetable oil

steps:

  1. line two baking sheets with paper towels. set aside.
  2. to a 7 to 8-quart pot, add 3 quarts water, the distilled white vinegar kosher salt, and baking soda, bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. peel and cut the potatoes into 1/4 x 1/4 inch thick matchsticks that are each about 3-1/2 inches.
